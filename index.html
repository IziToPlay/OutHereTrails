Who Are We?

Out Here Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views
of the Pacific Ocean. Your stay at Out Here Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt and
a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

Things To Do

  • Unwind in the heated outdoor pool and whirlpool.

  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours.

  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers and beverages.

  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view.

  • Learn rock climbing and test yourself in our variety of routes.

  • Explore the incredibles trails and the unique ecosystem that surrounds them.
